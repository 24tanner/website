Moving in and out

If you are moving in or out of AKM, please contact Phil. Phil will provide advice on where removalist trucks can park and ensure there is minimum disruption and inconvenience to other residents and care is taken not to damage the building.

Care should be taken to ensure the flooring, walls and lift are not damaged during the move. The lift doors should not be held open for extended periods of time.

Please do not park in front of private garage doors on Gibsons Way without prior approval from owners.
