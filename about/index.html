Useful information

Please help us maintain the amenity of our beautiful heritage building and help safeguard everyone’s investment.

Rubbish

The skips in the garage are for household waste only and hard rubbish cannot be put in the skips.

If you have hard rubbish, please contact the council. You can request a maximum of 2 hard rubbish collections per calendar year.

Hard rubbish collection can be arranged with Yarra Council by :

calling: 9205 5555
emailing: info@yarracity.vic.gov.au

see: https://www.yarracity.vic.gov.au/services/recycling-and-rubbish/hard-rubbish

Residents or lot owners will be charged for dumping hard rubbish and  cardboard in the garage and skips.

Please place all recycling in the yellow topped wheelie bins. Please ensure all recycling is flattened or broken down as much as possible.

Security

CCTV has been installed in the building to improve security for residents.

If you have any concerns regarding security, lost or stolen items from the garage, see non-residents in the garage or common property areas, please contact the Owners Corporation Manager.

Insurance

If you would like to make an insurance claim on the Body Corporate insurance, please contact the Owners Corporation Manager.

All insurance claims must be approved by the Owners Corporation Committee to minimise risks to insurance premiums.

Building works

Any proposed building or structural works on a lot (including car parks) or affecting the common property must be sent to the Owners Corporation Manager, for approval by the Owners Corporation Committee.

Please refer to the full Owners Corporation rules for details.
